## Definition

The divergent boundary means move apart. At this boundary, the plates are moving away from each other.

## Also Known As

- Constructive boundary

## Characteristics

- New ocean floor is formed
- Ocean floor spreading occurs
- Earthquakes do **not** typically occur on these faults
- Can form volcanoes above and below ground

![Continental-Continental Constructive Plate Boundary]({static}/images/Continental-continental_constructive_plate_boundary.svg_-300x180.png)

## Notable Divergent Locations

- Mid-Atlantic Ridge
- West Antarctic Rift
- Galapagos Rise

## Local Relevance

**Important Note:** Divergent boundaries are not found in the Coachella Valley.
