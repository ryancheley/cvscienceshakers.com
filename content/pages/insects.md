## Hemipteran Suborder Prosorrhyncha

Prosorrhyncha has two families/groups: Coleorrhyncha and Heteroptera

### Heteroptera Group
- 8 infraorders
- 79 families
- About 38,000 described species

### Coleorrhyncha
- 2-5 mm long and very flat
- Contains single family Peloridiidae

## Insect Species

### Stink Beetles
- **Scientific Name:** Eleodes obscurus
- **IUCN Status:** Unknown

### Boatmen Bugs
- **Scientific Name:** Corixidae
- **IUCN Status:** Unknown
