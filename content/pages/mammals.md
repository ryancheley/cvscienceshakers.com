### 1. Bighorn Sheep
- **Scientific Name:** Ovis canadensis
- **Group Name:** Herd
- **IUCN Status:** Least Concern

**Description:** Male bighorn sheep are known for their large curled horns used for mating rights. They ram each other at 20 miles per hour, fighting for hours with minimal injury due to their large skulls.
