The San Andreas Fault is probably the most famous fault in the nation, if not the world.

## Overview

The San Andreas Fault is where the North American and Pacific tectonic plates meet. This massive geological feature is:

- **800 miles long**
- **10 miles deep**

## Notable Earthquakes

- Magnitude: 7.9

### 2. 1906 San Francisco Earthquake

### 3. 1957 San Francisco Earthquake
