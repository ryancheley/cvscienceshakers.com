## What is a Thrust Fault?

A thrust fault slips at a **45 degree angle or less** from a normal reverse fault.

## Characteristics

- Sudden faults that are difficult to see
- Can remain dormant for extended periods

## Local Example

There is a thrust fault located at Whitewater Preserve.
